This "to do" list uses localStorage to save the user's input even after they have left the page. You may add as many tasks as needed and when they have been checked off, the tasks are moved to the "completed" section.

You may add a task by either pressing on the button labeled with a plus sign, or pressing enter on the keyboard while the focus is on an input box.

Macs is a fictional restaurant that I created to demonstrate how I might design for a client. With the exception of the food images and the UI button SVGs, I designed everything in this concept site. Users may browse food items and go through an online order process. The site accepts input towards the end of the "transaction", but nothing is done with the information that is entered.
